## Claim Code Privacy

Claim codes are cryptographic secrets delivered via email. The email serves only as a transport mechanism — no email-to-wallet mapping is stored or required at any point in the claim process. Possession of the claim code is the sole authentication requirement, preserving complete financial privacy for all parties. Not even the sender can determine which wallet address claimed their transfer — only that it was claimed.
